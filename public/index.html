I am an assistant professor in the Department of Computer Sciences at the University of Wisconsin - Madison since 2025. I am also affiliated with the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering. Previously, I went to CMU for my Ph.D. and worked with Prof. Anthony Rowe and Prof. Swarun Kumar.

My research interests broadly lie in wireless systems and cyber-physical systems. My goal is to build wireless systems that perceive and communicate with high fidelity unlocking new application potentials.

In pursuit of this vision, we have built systems along the following themes:

  • Learning driven radio frequency systems (ICCV 25, CVPR 24, ICRA 23)
  • New wireless communication paradigms (MobiCom 24, MobiCom 21, MobiCom 21)
  • Wireless + Robotics (ICRA 25, Sci Ro 24, IROS 22)
  • Embedded applications of wireless signals (MobiSys 20, UbiComp 21, IPSN 23)

If you are interested in graduate or undergraduate research, and these or allied topics seem interesting to you, please shoot me an email!
